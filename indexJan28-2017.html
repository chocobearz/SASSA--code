SASSA

Statistics and Actuarial Science Student Association

SASSA is the official student organization serving SFU's Statistics and Actuarial Science Undergraduate Students. We are dedicated to serving all students by creating a friendly and positive community, providing study groups, and networking among faculty.

    The Spring 2017 Executive Team



    President
    Christopher Kevin
    Christopher Kevin is third year Actuarial Science Student. He has passed 2 SOA preliminary exams and has Actuarial co-op experience in iA Financial Group. In addition to his studies and working experience, Christopher was the Council Representative for SASSA in 2016/2017 year and he also serves as a SOA Center of Actuarial Excellence (CAE) Student Summit Representative for SFU in 2016.

    As President, Christopher’s main goal are to promote and encourage Statistics and Actuarial Science students to take a proactive role in membership and bringing unique growth to SASSA.
    Vice-President
    Nathan Esau
    Nathan Esau is a fourth year Actuarial Science Student. He has passed 4 SOA preliminary exams and has co-op experience working as an actuarial programmer at GGY, an undergrad research assistant at SFU and a methodologist at Statistics Canada. Nathan has also participated in a number of team case competitions. He represented SFU at the Munich Re Cup in 2016 and was a finalist at the ASNA competition in 2016.

    As vice president, Nathan hopes students can make new friends and build connections through SASSA. It is his last semester at SFU, and he hopes SASSA students will try to stay connected after they graduate.
    Secretary
    Richard Li
    Richard Li is a third year Actuarial Science Student. He has passed 1 SOA preliminary exam and works as a math peer tutor for the physics department.

    The main goal for Richard is to support the president in ensuring the smoothing function of SASSA and keep formal records of every general meeting for the student union organizer.
    Treasurer
    Forrest Zhu
    Forrest Zhu is a second year Actuarial Science Student. He has co-op experience in accounting for a private enterprise and has passed one SOA preliminary exam. Forrest was the treasurer in 2016 and will continue to support SASSA as a treasurer in the upcoming year.

    As treasurer, Forrest is entrusted by the members to know the ins and outs of SASSA finances and be the liaison person to communicate with Simon Fraser Student Society.
    ACMA Department Representative
    Jenny Li
    Jenny Li is a fourth year Actuarial Science Student. She has passed 4 SOA preliminary exams and has co-op experience working as an actuarial co-op student at WorkSafeBC, an undergrad research assistant at SFU and a business development intern at PwC China. This spring Jenny is working for the second time as a TA in the Statistics Workshop. In the past she has also volunteered at Students Offering Support as an economics tutor.

    As departmental representative, Jenny would like to build connections between the ACMA professors and students. She plans to help organize an actuarial afternoon event where current students can network with alumni working in the Greater Vancouver Area.
